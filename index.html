What this is

Coding used to give you the edge. It doesn't anymore.

The hard part has shifted. It's no longer coding — it's deciding what gets built and what doesn't. The people who stay relevant aren't the ones who learn the next tool — they're the ones in the room when that decision gets made.

Where AI stops
  • AI can list every option. It can't decide which one you'd actually stand behind.
  • AI can tell you which way to go. You need to understand what each path costs.
  • AI can write the recommendation. It can't answer for it when things go wrong.
  • AI can spot a problem. It can't tell you if it's the right one to solve.
  • AI can prepare you for the meeting. You still have to know what you're talking about.
